The Best Marvel Movies by Navin Selvaraj.

My top 3 movies of all time

Avengers: Infinity War

Avengers: Infinity War" is a superhero film released in 2018, based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers. It is the third Avengers film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and is directed by Anthony and Joe Russo. The film serves as a direct sequel to "The Avengers" (2012) and "Avengers: Age of Ultron" (2015). The main plot of "Infinity War" revolves around the villain Thanos (played by Josh Brolin) and his quest to collect all six Infinity Stones, which are immensely powerful objects that grant their bearer incredible abilities. Thanos believes that by obtaining all the stones and placing them in the Infinity Gauntlet, he can bring balance to the universe by wiping out half of all life, which he sees as a solution to overpopulation and resource scarcity. The film brings together numerous characters from the MCU, including the Avengers, the Guardians of the Galaxy, Doctor Strange, Black Panther, Spider-Man, and more, as they unite to stop Thanos from carrying out his genocidal plan. The heroes face intense battles on various fronts as they try to prevent Thanos from collecting all the Infinity Stones. "Avengers: Infinity War" is known for its epic scale, action-packed sequences, and emotional depth as characters grapple with the gravity of the situation and make difficult choices. The film ends on a cliffhanger, setting up the events of the follow-up film, "Avengers: Endgame," which was released in 2019. The film received critical acclaim for its storytelling, visual effects, and performances, and it was a major box office success, becoming one of the highest-grossing films of all time

Avengers: Endgame

Avengers: Endgame" is a superhero film released in 2019, based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers. It is the fourth Avengers film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and is directed by Anthony and Joe Russo. The film serves as a direct sequel to "Avengers: Infinity War" (2018) and concludes the overarching storyline that had been building up in the MCU for over a decade. The main plot of "Endgame" follows the aftermath of the devastating events of "Infinity War," in which the villain Thanos succeeded in collecting all six Infinity Stones and used them to wipe out half of all life in the universe. The surviving Avengers and their allies are left to deal with the consequences and the grief of their losses. The film revolves around the Avengers' plan to undo Thanos's snap and restore the universe to its previous state. The team embarks on a time-travel mission to collect the Infinity Stones from various points in the past to create their own Infinity Gauntlet. This mission gives them a chance to revisit key moments from the MCU's history and interact with their past selves. "Avengers: Endgame" is known for its epic scale, emotional depth, and the culmination of character arcs that had been developed over multiple films. The film features a remarkable cast of characters, including the original Avengers like Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye, as well as newer characters like Doctor Strange, Black Panther, and Captain Marvel. The film concludes with an intense battle against Thanos and his forces, during which all the heroes of the MCU unite to fight for the fate of the universe. "Endgame" provides closure to several character arcs while also setting the stage for the future of the MCU. The film was highly anticipated and received critical acclaim for its storytelling, emotional weight, action sequences, and performances. It became a massive box office success, shattering numerous records and becoming one of the highest-grossing films of all time.

Thor Ragnarok

Thor: Ragnarok" is a superhero film released in 2017, based on the Marvel Comics character Thor. It is the third installment in the Thor film series and is part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The film is directed by Taika Waititi and stars Chris Hemsworth as Thor. The movie's plot is centered around Thor's quest to prevent the prophesied destruction of Asgard, his home realm. The title "Ragnarok" refers to an event in Norse mythology that signifies the end of the world and the death of various gods. In the film, Thor learns that his long-lost sister Hela (played by Cate Blanchett) has returned and intends to conquer Asgard. Thor is initially captured and banished to the planet Sakaar, where he becomes a gladiator and is forced to fight in a deadly arena. On Sakaar, Thor reunites with his fellow Avenger, the Hulk (played by Mark Ruffalo), who was last seen in "Avengers: Age of Ultron" (2015). Together, they form a plan to escape Sakaar, return to Asgard, and stop Hela's reign of destruction. Along the way, they gather allies including Valkyrie (played by Tessa Thompson) and Loki (played by Tom Hiddleston). "Thor: Ragnarok" is notable for its humor, vibrant visual style, and departure from the more serious tone of the previous Thor films. Director Taika Waititi infused the film with his signature comedic and quirky sensibilities, which garnered praise for revitalizing the character and the franchise. The film's comedic elements, action sequences, and character dynamics were well-received by both audiences and critics. "Thor: Ragnarok" contributed to the overall development of the Marvel Cinematic Universe and Thor's character as he grapples with his identity, responsibilities, and familial relationships
